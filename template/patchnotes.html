Patch 25.08 Notes

April 6, 2025

Here comes patch 25.08! Bringing balance changes across all active queues. This is the final patch of the current Ranked Season—make sure to get your 15 wins to lock in Victorious Twisted Fate! Improvements to Cryptbloom, Opportunity, and Overlord's Bloodmail. Mel and Zoe are making their meta return. Annie receives tweaks to shift damage from Tibbers' sustained damage to her spellcasts. Ahri and Syndra's builds are also being refined to match intended playstyles. Gwen and Yorick receive follow-up balancing as well.
